Idea 03 - Education RAG

University RAG Chatbot

A grounded assistant that answers student questions using official university documents, course catalogs, policies, and campus procedures.

The problem

Students often ask repeated administrative questions and receive inconsistent answers from scattered pages, PDF documents, and office queues.

What we build

A chatbot that gives concise answers with citations, handles student-service questions, and routes uncertain cases to the correct office.

How it works

Official documents are cleaned, chunked, embedded, and retrieved before generation. Responses are constrained by source passages and confidence thresholds.

Research angle

The paper can measure hallucination reduction, retrieval quality, source citation usefulness, and response acceptance by students.

Why the university benefits

It improves student support while keeping institutional knowledge private, auditable, and cheaper than repeated manual responses.
