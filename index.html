04 - Our Plan

Three paths, one outcome.

We're not betting everything on one company saying yes.

Plan A

Siemens track

Pass the Siemens interviews as a team. If accepted, we get the project idea and full technical support.

Plan B

Own idea + sponsor

We brainstorm an original idea and pitch it for sponsorship from Ejada, RAYA Tech, Microsoft, or Synapse.

Plan C

Independent build

If no sponsorship comes through, we execute our own idea independently.

Constant: a working prototype and research paper on the project.

06 - Our Project Ideas

Five concepts. Any one of them is buildable.

The final direction depends on the sponsorship path, but the concept set stays fixed and ranked.

01

StudyX

A structured revision workspace that turns notes, PDFs, and recordings into summaries, flashcards, and quizzes.

 02

Local LLM & RAG for University

A campus knowledge assistant grounded in university policies, course catalogs, and academic documents.

 03

Local AI Agent for SMBs

A private on-premise assistant for small businesses that need automation without sending data off-site.

 04

AI Immune System

An imaging assistant for anomaly detection and classification in blood cell imagery.

 05

Sami'ee (سميع)

Sign-to-Egyptian-Arabic hardware that translates gestures into text and speech.
