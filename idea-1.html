Idea 01 - Enterprise AI

Local AI Agent for SMBs

A private on-premise assistant for small and medium businesses that need workflow automation without moving sensitive records into external AI platforms.

The problem

Most SMBs have useful data scattered across documents, spreadsheets, emails, and simple CRMs. Cloud AI tools are hard to trust when the data includes customers, invoices, or internal procedures.

What we build

A local agent that answers business questions, drafts routine documents, retrieves internal knowledge, and automates simple back-office workflows from an on-premise machine.

How it works

Documents are indexed locally, retrieved through a vector database, and passed to a locally hosted model. The assistant exposes a browser UI and controlled tool actions.

Research angle

The paper can evaluate privacy-preserving deployment, latency, answer faithfulness, and cost compared with hosted LLM workflows for small organizations.

Deployment options

  • Entry tier: single workstation for document Q&A.
  • Office tier: shared mini server for multiple staff accounts.
  • Advanced tier: GPU workstation for heavier agent workflows.
