Slide 1: The Problem
Immunology Diagnostics Inefficiency & Bottlenecks
The Current State
Clinical laboratories and hematologists still rely on manual microscope reviews or baseline flow cytometry to analyze blood smears and immune responses.
Technical Friction
Manual workflows suffer from fatigue-driven error rates and inter-observer variability, especially with atypical white blood cell shapes and micro-patterns.
The Core Dilemma
Healthcare facilities are forced to choose between fast but shallow manual scanning and expensive specialized testing that takes more time and money.

Slide 2: What We Build
ImmunAI Recognition Engine

A high-throughput, web-accessible intelligent software engine designed to automate, segment, and classify microscopic immunologic data.

1. Automated Cellular Ingestion
Normalizes, balances, and segments high-resolution blood smear micrographs without destroying fine semantic boundaries.
2. Deep Learning Segmentation
Uses custom convolutional networks optimized to isolate complex cell structures and morphometry.
3. Diagnostic Analytics Interface
Generates real-time interactive differentials with cell-by-cell abnormality metrics on a clean dashboard.
