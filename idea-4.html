Idea 04 - Medical Vision

AI Immune System

A computer vision assistant that detects and labels anomalies in blood cell imagery to support faster clinical screening workflows.

The problem

Manual cell screening is time-consuming and depends on specialist availability. Delays can slow down diagnosis and follow-up decisions.

What we build

A web tool where a microscope image is uploaded, analyzed, annotated, and returned with detections and confidence scores for review.

How it works

A detection model identifies target cell classes and anomalies. A backend stores cases, annotations, and model outputs for comparison and review.

Research angle

The paper can evaluate detection accuracy, dataset quality, model explainability, and clinician-in-the-loop review workflows.

Data strategy

Start with public blood-cell datasets, then seek a supervised hospital or lab partnership for validation images and annotation review.
