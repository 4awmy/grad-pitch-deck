Student Study Workflow Friction

Students collect too much material and often revise passively. Turning lectures into useful practice takes time they do not have.

Notes, PDFs, and recordings are scattered across tools, so revision becomes manual and inconsistent.

The Core Dilemma

Students either re-read everything or use general tools that do not keep course context or weak-topic tracking.
