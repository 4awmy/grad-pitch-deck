Idea 05 - EdTech Platform

StudyX

A study workspace that converts lecture notes, PDFs, and recordings into summaries, flashcards, quizzes, and revision plans.

The problem

Students collect too much material and often revise passively. Turning lectures into useful practice takes time they usually do not have.

What we build

A web app that turns course material into structured notes, flashcards, short quizzes, and revision sessions tied to a student's upcoming exams.

How it works

Uploaded content is parsed, summarized, chunked by topic, and converted into question formats. Student performance updates the revision plan.

Research angle

The paper can study generated-question quality, retention improvement, personalization, and student engagement across a semester.

Differentiation

Unlike a general chatbot, StudyX keeps course context, tracks weak topics, produces repeatable practice, and gives students a revision loop instead of one-off answers.
